About PakDebit

Our Story

PakDebit was founded in 2023 with a vision to revolutionize banking in Pakistan by offering Sharia-compliant financial services that combine modern technology with ethical principles. Our journey began when our founders recognized the need for a banking institution that could serve the growing population of Pakistanis seeking Riba-free financial solutions without compromising on convenience or accessibility.

From our humble beginnings with just one branch in Karachi, we've expanded to over 50 branches across Pakistan, serving more than 500,000 customers. Our commitment to innovation has led us to develop Pakistan's first completely digital Riba-free banking platform.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, ethical, and innovative banking solutions that empower individuals and businesses across Pakistan to achieve their financial goals while adhering to Islamic financial principles.

Our Values

At PakDebit, we are guided by four core values:

  • Integrity: We conduct all our operations with transparency and honesty, ensuring complete compliance with Islamic banking principles.
  • Innovation: We continuously invest in cutting-edge technology to provide our customers with seamless banking experiences.
  • Inclusivity: We believe banking should be accessible to all Pakistanis, regardless of their location or financial status.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in every aspect of our service, from customer support to financial products.
